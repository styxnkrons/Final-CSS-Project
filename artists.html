Our Staff

Here at Red Rose Studios, our staff are family.




Kat Von D


Starting at the age of 16, Kat has been a staple of the tattooing world for 22 years. With a mountain of experience and a unique style that mixes both contemperary punk and gothric styles, Kat is ready to give you the tattoo of your dreams.




Ed Hardy


Ed Hardy has become synonymous with his clothing brand, but it is often forgotten that he remains one of the best tattoo artists in the business. With over 50 years of experience, he has worked with clients across the country.




Paul Booth


Paul Booth is a name known not just for his work as a tattoo artist, but as a musician as well. Due to his ties to the industry, he has worked on some of the biggest names in music like Adam Levine of Maroon 5, Justin Bieber, Miley Cyrus, and Cory Taylor.
